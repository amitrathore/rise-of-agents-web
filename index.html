The Rise of Agents

A Visual Guide to The Age of Abundance

Rise of Agents documents the end of the "Job" and the birth of a new, agentic economy. This visual field guide translates the complex shift from a world of scarcity into a story of human abundance and autonomous intelligence.

It maps the transition from selling time to directing intent: a future where agents do the work and humans own the outcomes.

That future is now.

Jobs Genesis

Jobs

The era of selling your time to a ledger is ending. As autonomous intelligence drives the marginal cost of labor toward zero, the traditional "Job" is seen as a temporary coordination mechanism that has finally outlived its purpose.

The shift from being a line item on a corporate balance sheet to becoming a sovereign owner of an agentic system is not just a technological change; it is a fundamental re-orientation of human agency.

The advice for the coming age is clear: stop competing with the machine for the role of "worker" and start mastering the role of "architect." In the post-employment economy, value is no longer found in the hours you spend, but in the intent you direct and the infinite games you choose to own.

Genesis

For centuries,
the world was managed
by a Static Ledger.

It was a book of history.
A record of what was already gone:
Your time, your labor, and your costs.

In that world,
you were a line item on a balance sheet.
A "resource" to be spent.
A "cost" to be managed.

But the books no longer balance.

When intelligence
becomes a utility—
like water,
like light—
the marginal cost of "doing"
drops to zero.

The heavy, gray office of the past
was built on Scarcity.
It was a world where
human labor was the only way
to turn the wheel.

The glowing garden of the future
is built on Abundance.
It is a new substrate
where agents do the work,
and humans own the outcomes.

We are moving
from a life of selling our time
to a life of directing our intent.

The Wisp rising from the page
is not a tool.
It is a unit of autonomy.

It is the end of the "Job"
as a coordination mechanism.
It is the beginning
of the Infinite Game.

The old books are closed.
The new ones are being written in light.

Welcome to the Rise of Agents.

Are you Human?

The Rise of Agents marks a shift from humans pushing the system to humans shaping it. Networks of autonomous intelligence now carry the weight of execution, while people stand at the edge of the map, choosing where to direct intent and how value should flow. This is not an upgrade to the old economy; it is a new landscape where ownership, coordination, and creativity are redesigned around agents from the ground up
